*10000Pa powerful suction fan blower: Data tested by the manufacturer. With a full charge and in turbo mode, the robot vacuum can achieve suction of up to 10,000Pa. Results may vary due to different usage conditions.
*5200mAh is the nominal battery capacity.
*180-minute: Data tested by the manufacturer. A sample robot vacuum was charged to full and tested on an open tile surface at different power levels. The working time was recorded until the robot vacuum initiated its return and the total cleaning area was displayed in the Xiaomi Home app. Under these conditions, the robot can vacuum and mop for ≥180 minutes in standard mode with a single run. Actual performance may vary depending on usage conditions, floor type and other factors.
*Anti-tangle: Refers to a reduction in hair tangling in the main and side brushes during vacuuming. Hair tangling will vary in different environments.
*520mL dust box: The dust box was filled with small plastic particles until it overflowed, then an electronic scale was used to measure the weight. After three tests, results showed that the dust box capacity was ≥520mL. Actual results may vary due to different usage conditions.
*270mL water tank: The water tank was filled with water and then the water was poured into a measuring cup to acertain the water tank's capacity. The capacity was measured to be ≥270mL. Water tank capacity may vary by up to 5% error, which is within the normal range.
*20mm: Data tested by the manufacturer. When the mop pad is dry, the robot vacuum can overcome steps with a height of up to 20mm. Actual results may vary due to different usage conditions.
*Xiaomi Home app (V6.7.200) is currently only compatible with iOS10.0 and above. For the best user experience, please update your device to the latest software version.
